Objective

Our brief was to create instantly recognizable packaging for a well known bakery in Derry City.

Summary

Using advanced 3D visualization, we were was able to give the guys at yum cakes instant, photo realistic images of what each iteration of their design would look like before sending to print. This allowed us to work collaboratively on the design and make sure it was perfect before sending to the printers (and avoiding any nasty, expensive surprises in the process).
